# ECONOMICS.md

## What's a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others. A typical deal involves a startup switching some portion of their AI spend to Credex-sourced credits.

**Assumptions (conservative):**
- Average startup AI spend: $800/month
- Credex captures 30% of that spend as credit sales: $240/month per customer
- Gross margin on credits: ~25% (discount arbitrage)
- Gross profit per customer per month: $60
- Average customer lifetime: 18 months
- **LTV per converted customer: $60 × 18 = $1,080**

A high-savings lead (>$500/mo identified waste) has higher LTV potential — they're spending more, so there's more to capture. Conservative LTV for high-savings leads: **$1,500–$2,500**.

## Conversion Funnel

```
Visitor lands on leakr.ai
        ↓ 40% complete audit
Audit completed
        ↓ 25% submit email via lead modal
Email captured
        ↓ 20% of high-savings cases book Credex call
Credex consultation booked
        ↓ 40% convert to credit purchase
Credit purchase (revenue)
```

**Funnel math for 1,000 visitors:**
- 400 audits completed
- 100 emails captured
- ~15 high-savings cases (>$500/mo savings found)
- 3 Credex consultations booked
- 1–2 credit purchases
- Revenue per purchase: ~$240/month × 18 months = $4,320 gross revenue per customer
With ~25% margins, estimated LTV is closer to ~$1,080.

Even with rough assumptions, the funnel can work if acquisition stays mostly organic.

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months

Credex needs to generate $1M ARR from credit sales driven by Leakr leads.

**Working backwards:**
- $1M ARR = ~$83,333/month in credit revenue
- At $240/month per customer: need **347 active paying customers**
- At 40% conversion from consultation to purchase: need **868 consultations**
- At 20% conversion from high-savings lead to consultation: need **4,340 high-savings leads**
- At ~15% of audits being high-savings: need **28,933 audits completed**
- At 40% visitor-to-audit conversion: need **72,333 visitors** over 18 months
- That's ~4,000 visitors/month — very achievable with consistent content + HN + organic sharing

**What has to be true:**
1. Leakr gets to 4,000 visitors/month by month 6 — requires consistent HN/Reddit presence and the shareable URL viral loop working
2. Audit-to-email conversion stays at 25%+ — requires the lead modal timing and copy to be right (value shown first)
3. Credex sales team can handle 50+ consultations/month by month 12
4. Average credit purchase is $240/month — requires Credex to close mid-size startups, not just solo founders

**The most fragile assumption:** the 40% consultation-to-purchase conversion. If Credex's sales process is weak, the whole funnel leaks at the bottom. Leakr can only control the top of the funnel.
