## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No credit card, no account, no catch. We show you the audit instantly. We ask for your email after — never before.

**Q: How accurate is the pricing data?**
A: Every number in our audit engine traces to an official vendor pricing page, verified the week of submission. We cite every source in our PRICING_DATA.md.AI pricing changes fast so the pricing data was verified during the week of submission.

**Q: Will you share my data?**
A: The public share link strips your email and company name. Only tool names and savings numbers are shown publicly. Your email goes to Leakr and - for high-savings cases -to Credex, who may reach out about discounted credits.

**Q: What's Credex?**
A: Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — same tools you're already using, at a lower cost. They source credits from companies that overforecast. If Leakr finds you're spending $500+/month, Credex is often the fastest way to capture those savings.

**Q: What if my stack looks fine?**
A: We'll tell you that honestly. "You're spending well" is a valid audit result. We don't manufacture savings. You can sign up to be notified when new optimisations apply to your stack.
